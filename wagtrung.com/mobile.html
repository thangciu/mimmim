UI Dashboard Designs

For some security and branding reasons, some information will be blurred as well as not showing the entire UI made for the project. This is a support system to sell apartments online at online sales for customers who want to make a deposit or buy and sell in a certain period of time (Flash sale).

And In this UI case study, We provide a solution for the responsive UI part that helps increase the customer's apartment deposit by 20%, because not everyone who comes to the event brings a laptop with them. And choosing to change the display to adapt well on the phone application is the solution that Team brings to customers to increase the user experience and flexibility when using.
