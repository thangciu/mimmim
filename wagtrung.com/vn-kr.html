About 30VNKR

The contest is held nationwide by citizens of Vietnam and Korea to celebrate 30 years of diplomatic relations. Logo design contest, with nearly 800 participants with requirements for connectivity, bilateral, and peer-to-peer for the relationship between the two countries.

The Solutions

Ideas & implementation

Through the process of learning and researching about the culture of both Vietnam and Korea. I finally came up with 30 different ideas as well as representing the 30th anniversary. Ideas are developed on national flag colors, symbolic flowers, mascots, abstractions, landmarks… I bring a minimalist design solution that effectively shows on different printing conditions as well as degree

One of my designs was chosen as the main image for the activities to celebrate the 30th anniversary of the two countries, which also means that the creative solution for my design won the award. especially at this contest because it met the requirements of the meaning of succinct transmission and minimalist aesthetic.
